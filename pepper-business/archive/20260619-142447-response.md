## New findings

### N1 — Hardcoded **2024** Black-Friday CPM prices in a revenue path *(highest)*
- **Evidence:** `app/Services/Business/CpmPriceService.php:123-166` hardcodes BF date windows for **2024 only** (`2024-11-01..2024-12-02`) and per-country overrides — pre-BF: DE=14, MX=10, FR/GB/PL/NL=12; peak/BF: 13/15/20.
- **Why a dev could misclassify it:** the dates are fixed to 2024, so **in 2025/2026 none of these windows match** → the BF CPM overrides silently never apply. A developer could read it as "CPM BF pricing is broken/ignored" or as dead code.
- **Possible business intent:** seasonal BF pricing that is **re-set each year** (dates + prices updated every Black-Friday season); 2024 values may simply be the last season's, with 2025 handled by a config/MR not yet present — or intentionally left as historical.
- **Domain:** Finance / CPM pricing (revenue).
- **Question:** Are the Black-Friday CPM price overrides updated each season? Are the hardcoded **2024** values current/intended, or should 2025+ values have replaced them (and where is BF pricing meant to live)?

### N2 — Loyalty program is country-limited (and a "small business country" split)
- **Evidence:** `GenerateLoyaltyProgramInvoiceService::shouldSkipInvoiceGeneration()` (`:86`) **only generates loyalty invoices for {DE, NL, GB, FR, PL}** (skips all others). `StoreLoyaltyProgramBillingRequest::isNotSmallBusinessCountry()` (`:108`) returns true only for **Poland + Netherlands**.
- **Why a dev could misclassify it:** looks half-wired/inconsistent — two different country sets for loyalty (invoicing = 5 countries; a billing branch = PL/NL only); could read as a missing-country bug.
- **Possible business intent:** the loyalty program was **rolled out to specific markets**, and PL/NL have a distinct ("not small business") billing treatment by design.
- **Domain:** Finance / Loyalty.
- **Question:** Which markets is the loyalty program live in (loyalty invoicing is limited to DE/NL/GB/FR/PL)? And what is the "small business country" distinction that singles out **PL/NL**?

### N3 — Dead, deprecated Black-Friday **2022** frequency-cap path
- **Evidence:** `StoreLineItemValidate::getFrequencyCapConfig()` is `@deprecated`, **not referenced anywhere** (grep), and contains a stale **2022** BF window (`2022-11-20..29`) setting `max_impressions=10` for GB/DE/FR/NL/PL.
- **Why a dev could misclassify it:** reads like a live BF frequency-cap rule (country list + impression cap), but it's dead and 4 years stale.
- **Possible business intent:** superseded by `config('adManager.frequency_caps')`; left in as deprecated rather than removed.
- **Domain:** GAM / AdManager.
- **Question:** Confirm `getFrequencyCapConfig()` is dead (safe to delete) and that BF frequency caps are now fully config-driven — anything still relying on the old `max_impressions=10` rule?

### N4 — `countriesWithNoIAPI` is an empty placeholder
- **Evidence:** `CountryCodes::$countriesWithNoIAPI = [ /* empty */ ]` (`:45`), consumed by `ListsCaching::...` (`in_array($country, [])` → always false).
- **Why a dev could misclassify it:** looks like an unfinished feature flag / a list someone forgot to populate.
- **Possible business intent:** intentional placeholder for markets lacking the internal Pepper API (currently none qualify), or superseded.
- **Domain:** Integrations / list caching.
- **Question:** Is `countriesWithNoIAPI` an intentional empty placeholder (no market currently lacks the internal API), or should non-deal markets (US/SE) be listed there?
