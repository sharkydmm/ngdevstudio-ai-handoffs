# Loyalty Program commercial model — recorded as confirmed business logic (closed)

Branch: pp-na-adding-skills (committed `870b9a774`, pushed). No ticket.

## Recorded
Product-confirmed: the **Loyalty Program is a reverse/payout (self-billing) flow**, not standard advertiser invoicing.
- Standard (CPC/CPM): Pepper Business **charges advertisers** and collects revenue.
- Loyalty: contributors publish deals on the Core/B2C sites (Dealabs/Pepper), those deals generate ad revenue, and a **share is paid back to the contributor** (self-billing / payout invoice) as an incentive to keep posting high-quality deals.
- It's a **separate commercial product**, intentionally **live only in DE/NL/GB/FR/PL**. The `loyalty_program_invoice_threshold` existing for other countries **does not** mean loyalty is active there — **not a bug, not an incomplete rollout.**
