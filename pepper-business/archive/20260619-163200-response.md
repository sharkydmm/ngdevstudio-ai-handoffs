# Next single business-logic question — the loyalty program (commercial purpose + market scope)

Branch: pp-na-adding-skills. Discovery/question only — no repo changes.

**What I found.** Loyalty is a **parallel, first-class invoicing flow**, separate from standard advertiser invoicing, but its *commercial purpose* and *market scope* aren't explained anywhere in the repo.

**Where.**
- Two billing types: `App\Enums\BillingType` — `STANDARD` vs **`LOYALTY`**.
- A separate entity: `app/LoyaltyProgramBusiness.php`; a loyalty invoice's owner resolves to `LoyaltyProgramBusiness` (not a normal `Business`) — `Invoice::business()` (`app/Invoice.php:207-209`) switches on `BillingType`.
- A separate generation service: `app/Services/Invoice/GenerateLoyaltyProgramInvoiceService.php`, run by the `invoices:loyalty:generate` cron, with its own per-country `loyalty_program_invoice_threshold`.
- **Market scope:** loyalty invoices are generated **only for `{DE, NL, GB, FR, PL}`** (`GenerateLoyaltyProgramInvoiceService::shouldSkipInvoiceGeneration`, `:86`) — even though **all 10 countries** have a `loyalty_program_invoice_threshold` configured in `config/countries.php`.

**What I think it does.** It's a distinct billing track for "loyalty program businesses" — a separate entity type with its own invoices/thresholds/contributors and (per earlier PL/NL legal finding) its own company-registration/tax rules. It runs in 5 of the 10 configured markets.

**Why it looks unusual / is worth asking.** From code alone I can't tell **what the loyalty program *is* commercially** — who the `LoyaltyProgramBusiness` parties are, whether a loyalty invoice is a **payout to them** (vs a charge, like standard advertiser invoicing), and why it's live in only 5 markets while config exists for 10 (a scope mismatch that, on its own, looks like an incomplete rollout — but given the L1/ES precedents, is likely intentional). This is product/commercial history, not technical cleanup, so I'd rather ask than guess.

**Question for Martin:**
> What is the loyalty program commercially — who are the "loyalty program businesses" it invoices (and is a loyalty invoice a **payout to contributors** vs a charge), and why is loyalty invoice generation live only in **DE, NL, GB, FR, PL** when all ten markets have a loyalty threshold configured (i.e. is the 5-market scope intentional, and what's the history/commercial reasoning behind those markets)?
