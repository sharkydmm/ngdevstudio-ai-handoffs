## The one question (highest-value unresolved ambiguity)

**What I found.** A loyalty-program billing rule keyed on a "small business country" classification.

**Where.**
- `app/Http/Requests/Permissions/LoyaltyProgram/StoreLoyaltyProgramBillingRequest.php:107` — `private function isNotSmallBusinessCountry($country) { return in_array($country, [Poland, Netherlands]); }`
- Used at `:55` and `:80` of the same request: a billing field (a tax/VAT identifier) is **required** when `isVatRegistered == 1 || isNotSmallBusinessCountry($country)`.
- Related context: loyalty-program *invoicing* runs only for `{DE, NL, GB, FR, PL}` (`GenerateLoyaltyProgramInvoiceService::shouldSkipInvoiceGeneration`, `:86`).

**What I think it does.** For loyalty-program contributors, a tax field is normally only mandatory if the business is VAT‑registered — **except in Poland and Netherlands**, where it is mandatory regardless. The codebase calls PL/NL "**not** small business countries," which (by negation) implies the other loyalty markets (DE/GB/FR) **are** treated as "small business countries," where the tax field stays optional for non‑VAT‑registered contributors. It reads like a country-specific **tax/compliance** rule (e.g. a small-business / VAT-exemption regime).

**Why it looks unusual.** (1) The term "small business country" appears nowhere else and isn't defined — it's opaque business jargon. (2) It's phrased as a **negative** (`isNotSmallBusinessCountry` = PL/NL), so the default/"small business" set is implicit and counterintuitive (it would group large markets like DE/GB as "small business countries"). (3) It's a *different* country set (PL/NL = 2) from the loyalty-invoicing set (5), so the two look inconsistent.

**Question for Martin:**
> In the loyalty program, what is the "small business country" distinction — i.e. why are **Poland and Netherlands** classified as *not* small-business countries (making the tax/VAT field mandatory for their loyalty contributors), while the other loyalty markets (DE/GB/FR) are treated as small-business countries where that field stays optional unless the contributor is VAT-registered?
